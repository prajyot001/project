overview

Taro Borewell Submersible Pumps are used for agricultural and domestic water pumping needs across the India. They are called Submersible Pumps because they are installed in boreholes / bore wells, submerged in water.Taro Pumps’ products are renowned for their power efficiency and durability, made from cast iron components and feature CED coatings. Driven by a hermetically sealed motor, they pump water to the surface through the installed pipe work.
